About Me

Hi! My name is Natasha Adam and I currently attend Western Sydney University, studying a bachelor's degree in Business, majoring in Economics and Property.

At the age of 13, I had a peaked interest in all things software, programming, coding, game development and animation. With this peaked interest, I began touching base, starting off with learning my first programming language: Python. After continuous months of learning the fundamentals and concepts, I seeked for ways to develop more as a beginner by learning other languages, which I did almost effortlessly since Python taught me the basics, allowing me to apply those concepts to other languages fairly easily. Even at the sweet age of 19, I still look for other methods to write codes and learn all there is to conquer about programming and develop any software that I believe will help me in the professional realm through my coding skills.


In saying that, I hope to get to know you and if you want to get to know more about me, head onto my Contact's page or connect with me either on LinkedIn, email or a phone call's sweet (Whatever floats your boat!).
